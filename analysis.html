Qualitative Highlights — Programming Suggestions
What fellows are asking for
More interactivity in workshops. Multiple fellows requested more hands-on exercises and audience participation beyond lecture-style content.
Shorter workshop duration. Requests to shorten from 2 hours to ~1 hour, and to move events off late evening time slots.
More niche topics requested. Fellows want content on ethics in tech, science diplomacy, local government processes, and civil rights + tech intersections.
Bridge Buddies highly valued when attended. Fellows who attended reported meaningful cross-team learning — the barrier is scheduling, not content.
More Bridge Buddies requested. Several fellows wished there were more sessions and asked for agendas to be shared in advance.
Cross-team collaboration wanted. Fellows want more structured ways to interact with people outside their project team during programming.
